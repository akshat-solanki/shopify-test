# AI Product

## What This Component Is

A dynamic product card for Shopify-style storefront placements.

It is meant to help merchants present products consistently across browsing surfaces without relying on a full product page layout.

## What It Should Feel Like

- polished
- quick to understand
- easy to act on
- visually adaptable
- strong in both compact and richer placements

## Primary Surfaces

- collection grid
- carousel
- featured spotlight
- multi-width comparison view for design review

## Merchant-Facing Priorities

- clean defaults
- simple style selection
- reliable media behavior
- flexible CTA presentation
- clear product information controls
- consistent results on mobile

## What To Avoid

- too much technical language
- overly fragile motion
- settings that overlap without adding clear value
- layouts that only work in one container size
- copy that sounds like a prototype instead of a product
